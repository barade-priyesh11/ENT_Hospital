Common Throat Conditions

The Common Ear Conditions section provides an informative overview of prevalent ear-related health issues encountered by individuals seeking care at our ENT hospital.

Otitis Media

(ear pain, fluid drainage from the ear, fever, temporary hearing loss)

Inflammation or infection of the middle ear, It is particularly common in children.

Tinnitus

(Perception of sound, Persistent noise, Intensity)

The perception of ringing, buzzing, or other sounds in the ears without an external source.

Cerumen Impaction

(Ear discomfort, Temporary hearing loss, Itching or irritation)

cerumen impaction occurs when wax accumulates in the ear canal.

Otitis Externa

(Ear pain, Itching, Sensitivity to touch, Fever)

otitis externa is an infection or inflammation of the outer ear canal.
